How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe to move tiles.
  • Merge same-value, same-color tiles to create larger values.
  • Special red-blue wildcards can merge with any color of the same value.
  • Special converter tiles (⟳) will flip the color of adjacent tiles when merged.
  • Click row/column numbers or press number keys (hold Shift for columns) to change tile colors.
  • Tiles 32 and 256 change color when created!
