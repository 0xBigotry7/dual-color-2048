How to Play

Swipe or use arrow keys to move tiles. Match same-colored tiles to merge them!

★ = Wildcard (merges with any color)

◎ = Converter (changes adjacent colors)

When tiles with values of 32 or 256 are created, they change color!

Use color change buttons to flip colors of rows or columns (limited uses).
